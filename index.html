About

N.P.S HSR is a school run by our chairman, Mr.K.P Goplakrishna, our dean, Ms. Santhamma Gopalkrishna, our director, Ms Bindu Hari and our principle, Ms. Shefali Tyagi. The school has aproximately 1500 students in total. All students enjoy the daily co-scholastic periods and also enjoy the friendly inter-house competitions.

Houses

N.P.S schools have 4 houses: Challengers, Explorers, Pioneers and Voyagers. There are many inter-house competitions, which are mainly introduced to students in their 6th grade. Most of the races though, are introduced to first graders and are done every year from 1st grade to 5th grade.
